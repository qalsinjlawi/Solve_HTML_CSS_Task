cards

Playing cards are small, rectangular pieces of card stock used for various games and entertainment. They typically feature numbers or symbols on one side and a uniform design on the other. Playing cards are popular worldwide for leisure and skill-based games.

  • Standard deck contains 52 cards divided into four suits: hearts, diamonds, clubs, and spades.
  • Common games include poker, bridge, and solitaire.
  • Playing cards are also used for magic tricks and card.
